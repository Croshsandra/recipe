  3. 3. Simple Recipe to Try:

    4. Scrambled Eggs

    Ingredients:

    • 2 eggs
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil

    Instructions:

    1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them with a fork until the yolks and whites are combined.
    2. Heat the butter or oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
    3. Pour the beaten eggs into the pan.
    4. Stir gently with a spatula, pushing the eggs from the edges to the center.
    5. Cook until the eggs are set but still moist.
    6. < Season with salt and pepper, then serve.
